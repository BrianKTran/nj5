Testimony

Hi fam, I am a born again Christ Follower. I grew up in the arts of hip hope and I want to share my life and about what the Gospel has done for me. I grew up in a broken home, my parents often fought about financial struggles and my dad was a gambling addict. When I was 10 my mom took me to church where I grew up learning about Jesus. My parents divorced and both attempted suicide twice each. I got into smoking marijuana and eventually got into harder drugs before I finished college. I didn't know who Jesus really was until I nearly overdosed due to my drug habits I picked up in an attempt to escape my reality. But since I learned the True Gospel and what the Blood of Jesus means for me, I gave up marijuana and other drug use and devoted my life and gifts to glorifying Christ and spreading the Gospel. Please follow me on my soundcloud: transcend Worship and listen, like, share my new album Ode to the New. You won't regret it. I appreciate any support. I hope my story inspires other artists and Christians in their walk with Christ Thank you and God Bless!
